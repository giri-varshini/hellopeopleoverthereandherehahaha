Happy April Fool's day dear fool

We are glad to see you here. Everyone becomes a fool some or the other time. This is your turn to become one. Have a great time. At the end of every April fool's day count the number of people you fooled and the number of people by whom you were fooled by. Have fun with friends. April 1st is known for its fun, laughter nad enthusism. Do not try to miss it this year.
